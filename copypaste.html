ABOUT ME

My name is Karolina. Originally I’m from Bosnia, but I’ve been living in the Netherlands for most of my life. I’ve been drawing since kindergarten and I was so happy when the Apple Pencil came out, it made illustrating so much easier. I’ve been working as a psychologist since 2009 and I’ve got two little boys so my drawing time is limited. But it gives me so much joy and energy to share it on social media and in my shop! It’s also great to be able to do something for underprivileged children in Bosnia by donating 25% of my proceeds to charity. My favourite subjets to draw are mothers, children and power women. Hope you like my creations and don’t hesitate to ask me anything, I’m also available for personal illustrations and portraits!
